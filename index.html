F.A.Q.

"What program do you use to draw?"

- I primarily use Procreate, but I also use Paint Tool Sai 2. I'm currently learning Clip Studio Paint!

"I'm a writer and I'm having trouble visualizing my character. Can you help?"

- Absolutely! I provide consulations and design help on my Services page.

"Do you offer fully rendered artwork in the Character Style Sheets?"

- No, the sheets are just sketches to help you on your way. The concepts can be passed onto another artist for more 'official' rendering.
